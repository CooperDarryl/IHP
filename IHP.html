Privacy Notice

Please be aware that although we take your privacy seriously we are currently only able to encrypt your name on our database, the answers you provide are not encypted and as such we request you think about the amount of detail you provide in these.

When you answer a question about friends or family only use first names or nicknames, any questions about locations such as the places you like to visit or would like to live in, only provide town or city names, if asked about finances or debt only state rough amounts and do not mention bank, store or credit provider names.

We do not use cookies on this website but please be aware that our web host might and the information they may gather is controlled by them and not the Aldingbourne Trust.


* You will not be prompted on exiting the page if using an iPhone/Pad, please be cautious.

If you have any queries or would like to know more about how we process your data please contact us.
